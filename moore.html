Sir Roger Moore

Hello, Series Savior

Roger Moore was a new era for the series and proved it could survive past Connery. He brought a sense of playfulness and fun to the series that it had never had before. He was much more comedic and outlandish, one film even finding agent 007 in space. With seven films, he has the most movies to his name and he was knighted for his efforts.
